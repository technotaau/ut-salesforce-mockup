Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our Salesforce services and implementation process.

Do we need to buy Salesforce licenses before starting?

No. We'll help you determine the right licenses for your team size and needs, then handle procurement as part of our setup process. We can also help you understand Salesforce pricing and negotiate the best deal.

What if our team has never used Salesforce?

We provide comprehensive training tailored to each role—sales reps, managers, and admins. Most teams are confident users within 2 weeks. All training is recorded for future reference and new hires.

Can you integrate Salesforce with our existing tools?

Yes. We routinely integrate with QuickBooks, Mailchimp, DocuSign, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and 200+ other platforms. If it has an API, we can connect it.

What happens after go-live?

Every implementation includes 30 days of post-launch support for troubleshooting and adjustments. After that, we offer ongoing managed services or can train your team to manage Salesforce internally.

How is Salesforce priced?

Salesforce charges per user per month. Sales Cloud and Service Cloud start at $25/user/month (annual contract). We help you choose the right edition and features for your budget. Non-profits may qualify for significant discounts through Salesforce.org.

What if we already have Salesforce but it's not working?

We offer Salesforce rescue services—auditing your current setup, identifying issues, and rebuilding or optimizing as needed. Many of our clients come to us after failed DIY implementations.

Do you work with companies outside Houston?

Yes. While we're based in Houston and specialize in serving Texas organizations, we work with clients across the United States remotely.
